cricket
Cricket is a bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of eleven players each on a large field, centered around a 22-yard-long pitch. One team bats, trying to score runs by hitting the ball and running between the wickets, while the other team bowls and fields, trying to prevent runs and get the batters out. After both teams take turns batting and bowling, the team with the most runs wins.
